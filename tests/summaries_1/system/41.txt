SEOUL , South Korea _ U.S. President Bill Clinton won South Korea 's support Saturday for confronting North Korea over a suspected nuclear site , and he warned the North 's communist leaders not to squander an historic chance to make a lasting peace on the peninsula .
President Clinton made an unusual , direct appeal to North Korea on Saturday to set aside any nuclear ambitions in favor of strengthening ties to South Korea and the United States .
Concerns that North Korea was developing long-range missiles capable of striking all of Japan and parts of the United States escalated when North Korea fired a rocket on Aug. 31 .
