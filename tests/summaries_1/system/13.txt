Turkey wants Italy to extradite the rebel , Abdullah Ocalan , leader of the Kurdistan Workers ' Party , which is seeking Kurdish autonomy in southeastern Turkey .
Turkey has asked for his extradition and Ocalan has asked for political asylum .
Turkey considers Ocalan a terrorist and its most-wanted criminal .
Ocalan , whose Kurdistan Workers Party has waged a 14-year war with Turkey for autonomy in southeastern Turkey , is in prison custody in Rome where his arrest was announced Friday .
Turkey 's Anatolia news agency said Oz was determined not to free his hostage until Italy agreed to Turkey 's demand to send back Ocalan who is Turkey 's most wanted man .
