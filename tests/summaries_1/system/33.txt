The last time they were seen on the field at Yankee Stadium , they were inadvertently influencing the outcome of the game that sent the Cleveland Indians home and the Yankees to the World Series .
Chuck Knoblauch and Tino Martinez were as popular as squeegee men a week ago , the speculation rampant that one or the other or both might be exiled if the Yankees ' historic year crumbled in the post-season .
Since joining the Yankees , Martinez is 20 for 107 with one homer and 4 RBI in 29 post-season games .
So the Yankees will stay with Martinez , for better or worse , while he tries to stay positive and finally produce .
